## Share via link

You can share your dashboard via email with anyone in by clicking on the share button and sharing it as follows, if you would like a preview of the dashboard please click on the preview.

Your team members will be invited to your dashboard. By sharing your dashboard they will become a team member and can collaborate on future dashboards with you.
