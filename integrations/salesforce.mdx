This article guides you through the process of connecting Salesforce to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Salesforce data within the Upsolve platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Salesforce.

- User Name
- Password
- Security Token

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain the User Name and Password, go to the [article](https://support.omaticsoftware.com/s/article/Locating-your-Salesforce-Username-and-Password).

2. To obtain the Security Token, log in to your Salesforce account.

3. Click the profile avatar and choose Settings.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/salesforce-2.png" />

5. Check your email for the security token.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/salesforce-3.png" />

## Connecting Salesforce to Upsolve

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Salesforce" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source and input credentials you obtained in the previous step.

5. Click "Next" to create your Salesforce data source.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/salesforce-4.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Salesforce to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Salesforce data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
