This article guides you through the process of connecting Stripe to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Stripe data within the Upsolve platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need an API Key from Stripe.

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Go to this [link](https://stripe.com/) and log in to your Stripe account. This will take you to your Stripe dashboard.

2. Click the "Developers" button, as shown in the image below.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/stripe-1.png" />

3. In the Developers section, click the "API Keys" button.Then click the "Reveal test key" button to display your API Key.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/stripe-2.png" />

## Connecting Stripe to Upsolve

Now that you have your requirements, you can connect it to Upsolve using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Stripe" as your data source.

4. Enter your data source name and API Key.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/stripe-3.png" />

5. Click "Authorize" to create your Stripe data source.

Congratulations! You've successfully created a data source between Stripe and Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Stripe data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
