This article guides you through the process of connecting Instagram to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Instagram data within the Upsolve platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Instagram.

- Client ID (required for advanced setup)
- Client Secret (required for advanced setup)
- Business Account ID

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain Client ID and Client Secret, check this [article](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/facebook-login/guides/access-tokens/#clienttokens).

2. To obtain Business Account ID, check this [article](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1181250022022158?id=180505742745347).

## Connecting Instagram to Upsolve

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect Instagram to Upsolve using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Instagram" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source, input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/instagram-1.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" to authenticate and grant Upsolve access to your Instagram account.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Instagram to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Instagram data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
