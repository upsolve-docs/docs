This article guides you through the process of connecting Google Sheets to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Google Sheets data within the Upsolve platform.

Follow these detailed steps to establish a data source between Google Sheets and Upsolve:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal, select "Google Sheets" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source.

5. Click the "Signing in to Google Sheets" button. This will open a modal to allow Upsolve access to your Google Sheets account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/google-sheets-1.png" />

6. In the modal, click the "Allow" button to grant Upsolve access to your Google Sheets data.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/google-sheets-2.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Google Sheets to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Google Sheets data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
