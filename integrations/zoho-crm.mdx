This article guides you through the process of connecting Zoho CRM to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Zoho CRM data within the Upsolve platform.

Follow these steps to connect Zoho CRM to Upsolve:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Zoho CRM" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source and select a domain.

5. Click the "Signing in to Zoho CRM" button. This will open a modal to allow Upsolve access to your Zoho CRM account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/zoho-crm-2.png" />

6. In the modal, click the "Allow" button to grant Upsolve access to your Zoho CRM data.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/zoho-crm-1.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Zoho CRM to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Zoho CRM data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
