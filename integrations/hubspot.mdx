This article guides you through the process of connecting HubSpot to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your HubSpot data within the Upsolve platform.

## Connecting HubSpot to Upsolve

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal, select "HubSpot" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source name, click the Sign In button. This will open a modal to allow Upsolve access to your HubSpot account.

5. Allow Upsolve access to your HubSpot account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/hubspot-1.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected HubSpot to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your HubSpot data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
