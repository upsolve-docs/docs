This article guides you through the process of connecting LinkedIn Ads to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your LinkedIn Ads data within the Upsolve platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from LinkedIn Ads.

- Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
- Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)
- Default Account ID

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain the Client ID and Client Secret, go to the [LinkedIn developers site](https://www.linkedin.com/developer/apps).

2. Click Create App and complete the entire information. Specify your company name or create a new company page.

3. Click Create App. The App Settings is displayed.

4. Select the Auth tab.

5. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret.

6. To obtain the Default Account ID, [sign in to Campaign Manager.](https://www.linkedin.com/campaignmanager/).

7. The ads account name is in the upper-left corner of the page. The account ID number is below the account name.

8. To see your other ad accounts, click the account name in the upper-left corner of the page. The account ID number appears below each name.

9. For a detailed list of all your ad accounts, click the ads account name in the upper-left corner of the page, then click View all accounts at the bottom of the left menu. The account ID will appear below each account name.

10. Copy the Default Account ID.

## Connecting LinkedIn Ads to Upsolve

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "LinkedIn Ads" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source and input credentials you obtained in the previous step.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/linkedin-ads-1.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" to create your LinkedIn Ads data source and allow Upsolve access to your LinkedIn Ads account.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected LinkedIn Ads to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your LinkedIn Ads data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
