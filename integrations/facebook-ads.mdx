This article guides you through the process of connecting Facebook Ads to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Facebook Ads data within the Upsolve platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Facebook Ads.

- Default Ad Account ID
- Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
- Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain the Default Ad Account ID, refer to this [article](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1492627900875762).

2. To obtain the Client ID and Client Secret, refer to this [article](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/facebook-login/guides/advanced/manual-flow/#confirm).

## Connecting Facebook Ads to Upsolve

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect Facebook Ads to Upsolve using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Facebook Ads" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source, input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/facebook-ads-1.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" to create your Facebook Ads data source.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Facebook Ads to Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Facebook Ads data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
