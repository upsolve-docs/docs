This article guides you through the process of connecting Twilio to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Twilio data within the Upsolve platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need SID and Auth Token from Twilio.

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Go to this [link](https://console.twilio.com/).

2. Scroll down on the page, and you will find your Twilio SID and Auth Token, as shown in the image below.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/twilio-1.png" />

## Connecting Twilio to Upsolve

Now that you have your requirements, you can connect it to Upsolve using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Twilio" as your connection.

4. Enter a name for your data source name and paste values that you got before.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/twilio-3.png" />

5. Click "Authorize" to create your Twilio data source.

Congratulations! You've successfully created a data source between Twilio and Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Twilio data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
