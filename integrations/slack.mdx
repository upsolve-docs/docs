This article guides you through the process of connecting Slack to Upsolve, allowing you to seamlessly access your Slack data within the Upsolve platform.

Follow these detailed steps to establish a connection between Slack and Upsolve:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Upsolve.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Slack" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/slack-1.png" />

5. Click the "Signing in to Slack" button. This will open a modal to allow Upsolve access to your Slack account.

6. It will open a modal to grant permission to Upsolve.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/slack-2.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully created a new data source between Slack and Upsolve. You can now access and manage your Slack data seamlessly within the Upsolve platform.
